rule: warning

extensions: htm html flsnp

when: self MadCap:xref

test:

not self-ends MadCap:xref "!"
and not self-ends MadCap:xref "?"
and not self-ends MadCap:xref "."
and not self-ends MadCap:xref ","

message:

Misplaced punctuation in a cross-reference.  To fix: place punctuation
outside of the cross-reference element. *Note:* Flare replaces the
text in cross-references when it builds a target. Punctuation, and any
other text that you inserted inside this element could be replaced at
the next build.
